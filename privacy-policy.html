Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Daily Lotto. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information (name, email address, phone number, date of birth).
  • Payment details for transactions (processed securely).
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information.
  • Usage data, including interactions with our website and services.
  • Cookies and tracking technologies for personalized experiences.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To process transactions and provide lottery and betting services.
  • To verify your identity and age for legal compliance.
  • To improve our website experience and personalize content.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
  • To send promotional offers and updates (with your consent).

Data Protection & Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no online transmission is 100% secure, and we advise you to protect your account details.

Our security measures include encryption, firewalls, secure server infrastructure, and regular security audits to safeguard your information.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share information with:

  • Regulatory authorities for compliance purposes.
  • Payment service providers for secure transactions.
  • Third-party analytics services for website optimization.
  • Law enforcement if required by legal obligations.
  • Service providers who assist in website operations and customer support.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Cookies help us analyze site traffic, remember user preferences, and provide a more personalized experience. We use both session and persistent cookies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Request data portability.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable.
  • Restrict or object to the processing of your personal data.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please review this page periodically for changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@dailylotto.com.au.
